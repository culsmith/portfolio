Our Story


Scroll down to read!



Thanks for your investment in our love story! Here’s a recap, in case you’re newer to our lives (welcome!) or need a refresher (like we do, sometimes):



We officially met in an English class sophomore year, though in typical Hope College fashion, we had some overlap in social connections.
Cullen made the first move by inviting Lindsay to watch Apocalypse Now (1979) with him, the first assigned film of the semester. While it wasn’t a traditional movie date, it was a date nonetheless. And with the exception of a few days off-the-grid, we haven’t stopped talking since.



Cullen: I realized I loved Lindsay when I was in the Boundary Waters in northern Minnesota, on an annual trip with family. I was in the wilderness, totally disconnected from the rest of the world - and the way I missed her made me realize how much I wanted her in my life.



Lindsay: From our earliest conversations, I intuitively knew Cullen was different, and I had a sense that he could be in my life for a long time. That knowledge was revealed over months, then years - but it has always been a calm certainty. I first knew that I loved him when we decided to continue our relationship long-distance when I studied abroad. Neither of us considered letting this go.



Since then, we have walked together over long distances, through tragedies, across the graduation stage, into workplaces and graduate school, and along Lake Michigan as a newly-engaged couple. We now live in Ann Arbor, excited to see what’s in store in the next chapter of our story!


Cullen R. Smith 2019-2020
